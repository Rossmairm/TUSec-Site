Get ready to Hack Owl Night.
Owlhacks is a premier hackathon in Philadelphia organized by students from Temple University. In April, 500 students will come to the Science, Education & Research Center in Philadelphia to learn, collaborate and build over the course of 36 hours.

FAQ Answers to OWL your questions
When is Owlhacks?
Owlhacks will begin on the evenqing of Friday, April 10th at 8pm and will end with the at 4pm on April 12th.
Who can participate in Owlhacks?
All the awesomest student developers under the sun! Owlhacks is open for any student to attend - beginners included. If you're a student, make sure you register.
Why come to Owlhacks?
Owlhacks is an opportunity to grow as a creator, not just a developer. You'll be surrounded by some of the most creative and analytical minds in the country. Some of the most cutting-edge tchnologies will be on display, and by the end of the weekend, you could even win an award.
How do I get in?
The first step to getting into Owlhacks is applying. When you register, you submit a bunch of information about yourself that helps us decide if you'd be a good fit for Owlhacks. After we make a decision, we'll send you an email with the results.
Do Temple University students have to apply?
Nope! We will have internal applications just for Temple University students. Keep watching your inbox for more information.
How do I get involved if I'm not a student?
Only students can be hackers, but advisors are always handy. Shoot us an email if you're interested.
How much does Owlhacks cost?
Nothing! You don't need money to come to Owlhacks, just the willingness to learn and enthusiasm to create. You'll get free food and swag throughout the weekend - all you have to do is show up.
Who do I talk to if I have any questions?
If you have any questions or comments at all, don't hesistate to send an email to team@owlhacks.com
Are teams OWLlowed?
Yes, up to 4 people per team.
